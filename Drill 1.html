Cryptocurrencies of the World

Bitcoin (BTC)

The first and most popular cryptocurrency, created in 2009 by an unknown person using the name Satoshi Nakamoto.

Ethereum (ETH)

A decentralized platform that enables smart contracts and decentralizied applications (DApps) to run without downtime or fraud.

Litecoin (LTC)

A decentralized peer to peer cryptocurrency, Started in October 2011

Altcoins (alternative coins)

A digital currency and an alternative to Bitcoin
